Cloudiness

Cloudiness is more widespread, with highest concentrations at 0%, 90% and 75% respectively. Cloudiness is more spread out evenly at latitudes 0 and lower, but become more concentrated at the previous concentration levels (0%, 90% and 75%) at positive altitudes.
